  • The figures in the chart/table/graph reflects that___

prepositon

prep about change
  • increase+
    • in: in the number of___ ,
    • of: of+具体数值 eg: of +70% ;of + 500 (可以再跟in the number of___)
    • from: 表示变化 eg:from 100 to 130 units
    • by: 表示变化eg: (by) 30 percent;; decrease by 400 million
    • to: 表示到达 eg:to 130
prep about time
  • 在去年十二月: in December last year
  • 在2017年: in 2017
  • 在第二季度: in the second quarter
  • 1980至1981年间: between
  • 自2001年以来: since 2001
  • 在接下来的三个月里: over the following 3 months

  • It is clear from the figures/statistics
  • It is apparent from the figures/statistics
